About Prajwalan

History

Prajwalan, meaning "ignition" in Sanskrit, was first conceptualized in 2023 by the Association of Computer Engineers (ACE) at SRKR Engineering College. What started as a small inter-college coding competition has evolved into one of the most prestigious national-level hackathons in Andhra Pradesh.

Evolution

Over the years, Prajwalan has grown exponentially, attracting participants from across India. The event has become a platform for innovation, creativity, and technological advancement, fostering the next generation of tech leaders.

Mission

Our mission is to ignite the spark of innovation in young minds, providing them with a platform to showcase their technical prowess and creative problem-solving abilities while building lasting connections in the tech community.

2023

Genesis Edition

First-ever Prajwalan Hackathon held in March 2023. Organized by the ACE Club, CSE Dept. It was a 24-hour in-person coding event with 45+ teams from various colleges in Andhra Pradesh.

2024

National Expansion

Titled Prajwalan 2K24, the event saw a major scale-up with 500+ participants across India. The hackathon gained national recognition, and included problem-solving around tech, sustainability, and innovation.

About ACE Club
